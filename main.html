Our Story

"Made with passion, dedication, and love"

Romalyn's brings the authentic taste of the Philippines to Paris with our premium Ube. Sourced directly from Filipino farms, our Ube is carefully selected and processed to preserve its natural flavor and vibrant color.

Our commitment to quality and authenticity makes Romalyn's the perfect choice for food enthusiasts seeking novelty, creativity, and genuine Filipino flavors.
